## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum Reach

### Timing
✅ Post when your followers are most active (check Premium analytics)
✅ Avoid weekends (research shows lower engagement)
✅ Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM

### Engagement
✅ Reply to comments within first hour (boosts algorithm)
✅ Use emoji in threads (increases visual appeal)
✅ Quote tweet interesting replies
✅ Engage with replies from influencers

### Amplification
✅ After 4-5 hours, reply to top comment with additional insight
✅ Quote tweet one of your own posts the next day
✅ Reference other threads by day 3-4
✅ Share metrics once you have 24hr data

### Avoid
❌ Don't delete or edit tweets (ruins thread continuity)
❌ Don't over-reply (algorithm penalizes)
❌ Don't post same thread twice
❌ Don't spam hashtags (use 3-4 max)
